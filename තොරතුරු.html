Programme for enhancing B onion Production to meet the national requirement.

Posted By Agriculture Department

Although B onion production is successfully done in the country we are yet importing to meet the national requirement Intension of the President is to strengthen the local agriculture and put an end to crop production imports. The President has entrusted this enormous responsibility to the Ministry of Agriculture. All arrangements have been finalized to bring 5000 hactares under B onion cultivation in 2021. 25000 kg of seed B onion is required for implementing this under taking. Accordingly steps have already been taken to undertake seed B onion project in Digampathana area within the Agrarian Service Centre area of KimbissaDuring his inspection tour the Minister met farmer families who are contributing to the project. ‘’ Every family unit joining the project will be given material support to the value of Rs. 2.5 Million.

The Minister of Agriculture and the State Minister of Agriculture raided the fertilizer companies.

Posted By Agriculture Department

Agriculture Minister Mahindananda Aluthgamage and the State Minister Shashendra Rajapaksa unexpectedly visited the main store complexes of private fertilizer companies located in Peliyagoda and Kelaniya and observed the situation there. Although fertilizer shortage is experienced in certain areas of the country, private fertilizer companies are retaining fertilizer stock in their stores. Both Minister questioned the authorities the reason for not having taken action to issue fertilizer even at a minimum level..
